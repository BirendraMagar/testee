Our Advanced Dental Technologies

At 100SMILES, we stay at the forefront of dental innovation. Our state-of-the-art technologies ensure precise diagnoses, minimally invasive treatments, and superior patient care.

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution imaging with significantly less radiation for quicker, more accurate diagnoses.

Intraoral Cameras

Detailed views of your mouth for early detection and better treatment explanations.

Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive procedures with reduced pain and faster healing times.

CAD/CAM Technology

Custom dental restorations like crowns and veneers created in a single visit.

3D Printing

Accurate dental models and surgical guides for precise treatment planning.

Convenient virtual consultations from the comfort of your home.
